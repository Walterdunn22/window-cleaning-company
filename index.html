The Process of Professional Window Cleaning

Professional window cleaning companies typically follow a systematic process to ensure thorough and effective cleaning:

  1. Assessment: The cleaners assess the condition of the windows and identify any specific challenges or areas of concern.
  2. Preparation: Furniture and belongings near the windows are moved or protected to prevent damage during the cleaning process.
  3. Cleaning Solution: A suitable cleaning solution is applied to loosen dirt and grime from the glass surface.
  4. Squeegee Technique: Using a squeegee, the cleaners methodically remove the cleaning solution along with the dirt and grime, ensuring a streak-free finish.
  5. Detailing: Any remaining spots or streaks are addressed, and the edges and frames of the windows are wiped clean.
  6. Final Inspection: The cleaners perform a final inspection to ensure that every window meets their high standards of cleanliness.
